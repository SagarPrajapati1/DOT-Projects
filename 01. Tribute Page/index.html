Achievements

  • 1991- Create the linux kernel and the version control system Git
  • 2004- Named on the "100 Most Influential People in World" by Times magzine in 2004
  • 2010- Received an honory doctorate from the University of Helsinky in 2010
  • 2010- Received the 2010 Millennium Technology Prize and the 2010 Free Software Foundations Award for the Advancement of Free Software
  • 2013- Inducted into the Hall of Fame in 2013 on his contributions to the developement of Linux kernel and creation of Git
  • 2014- Inducted into the Hall of Fame of Computer History Museum in 2004
  • 2015- Received the 2015 IEEE Computer Society Computer Pioneer
  • 2019- Received the 2019 ACM SIGOPS Hall of Fame Award
  • 2021- Awarded the Takeda Foundation's 2021 Takeda Award for social contribution, which recognizes individuals who have made significant contributions to society through Technology
  • 2021- Received the 2021 IEEE MAsaru Ibuka Consumer Electronics Award, which recognizes individuals who made significant contributions to the feild of consumr electronics
